All About The Berkeley High Swim Team!

The Berkeley High School swim team is a very relaxed and fun team, full of nice people who are passionate about swimming.

 The coaches Veronica Dan and Bill are very nice people who enjoy seeing people progress in swimming.

Here are three reasons you should join the swim team:

  • Everyone is nice, the coaches promote positivity and only encouraging criticism.
  • It's not TOO competetive, so anyone can join and learn at their own pace without being pressured.
  • No need to tryout, all you need to do is sign up and give in a few forms of concent and medical forms!
